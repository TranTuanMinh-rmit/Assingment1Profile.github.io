Project Idea

The project that I have in mind is to make a mobile app that compiles a wide catalog of automobiles of as much manufacturers as can possibly be found with its overall specification and what they mean.

Motivation

Ever felt confused when a friend or family member talks about something you know that it's related to cars but you really don't understand? Ever felt discouraged when you want to get into the auto mobile world but felt ovewhelm by the abundance of information and how they are all scattered? This will help you with the situation above.

Description

The project, now named ‘Autodex’, will be mainly features as much known cars manufacturers as possible with at least five cars models for the last five years per manufacturer. Aside from car manufacturers’ name and their models, we will also be adding given specification of the manufacturers’ model.

Furthermore, to avoid more confusion, we will also be adding a segment dedicated to explain what we think will be confusing or need explanations. This application however, will not states the price ranges, where to get the best deals or do anything to impede in the manufacturers’ sales. This will be strictly a free library of car models and their manufacturers.

Tools and technology

This application will be focused on Android so the main tool will be Android Studio, the offical Android app maker. In the future, if possible, a comprehensive website will also be made using HTML5. iOS will be a possibility but not guaranteed.

Skills required

Android Studio usage is required for the mobile version while HTML, CSS and SASS to make the website.

Outcome

If the project is successful, it will solve the hassle of going all over the internet to find information regarding cars models and their manufacturers. It will also help newcomers to have a better understanding of a car's specification and their meaning
